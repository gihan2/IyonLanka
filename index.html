IYON LANKA IS...

Matara, a beautiful city in the southern province of Sri Lanka, is the economic and educational hub. Our company Iyon Lanka Foreign Employment Agency (PVT) LTD is locared in Matara city, which is easily accessible from all parts of the country. It is an agency that provides education and job training for migrant young people and Japanese language training, Japanase culture and Japanese discipline.
